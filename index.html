A BnB for everyone

Air Body BnB offers accomodation specifically designed for people with disabilities, one of the most popular accomodations of this BnB webpage. Open area living space and elevators make it possible for everyone to find their way through the house. A broad variety of local roads and flat paths through the surrounding meadows makes a stay at our accomodation wotrth every pennie.

Every guest will be met by our motto engraved above the main entry door:

To live is the rarest thing in the world. Most people exist, that is all.

Every design feature on the property and everything we do mirrors this motto. Everyone should have the same rights and be given the same opportunity to live happy and relaxed.

General information

Since the property is situated on the countryside, far away from the polluted air in the larger cities, a visit to Air Body Bnb will include lots of fresh air and a peaceful surrounding. Breakfast is usually served between 7 am to 10 am and is included in the lease. Lunch and dinner can be provided at a low additional cost. Morning and afternoon walks are arranged to comply with the needs of the guests. All rooms have free access to wi-fi.

History

Air Body BnB first opened up its doors for guest on a sunny day in June 1973. Back then the property only had three available rooms for rent, the three rooms on the first floor. Visitors where sparse during the initial five years but in 1979, after an article concerning countryside tourists' accomodation where published in a national newspaper, the BnB started to become more and more popular. A renovation in the 1980's made it possible to open up another three rooms for lease. These rooms, situated on the ground floor, where specifically designed for people with disabilities. The BnB's focus now shifted from common holiday guests from larger cities whom wanted to pay a visit to the countryside, to providing a sanctuary for people unable to walk by their own force. The garden and nearby walking paths where rebuilt to provide easier access for wheelchairs and are maintained in good condition.

Location

This BnB is situated in the north of England in North Yorkshire just outside York. North Yorkshire has much to offer if you are looking for peace and quiet. Lush vegetation mixed with large open fields made us choose this spot to open up our business. For more information about the county, please visit this page.

Room types

Two different kinds of rooms are available on this property: budget and luxury.

Budget Accomodation

Our three budget rooms have much to offer considering the low lease fee. In short, you get a single bed with plenty of room to move around with a wheelchair.

Included in all budget rooms are

  • A single bed
  • Plenty of space to move around with a wheelchair
  • A rural but functional design
  • Shared bahthroom
  • Breakfast buffet

Luxury Accomodation

The luxury bedrooms on this property have that extra bit of something. A larger bed and a larger living area with special design features makes these rooms irresistible.

Included in all luxury rooms are

  • A king-size bed or two single beds
  • Plenty of space to move around with a wheelchair
  • A luxurous, rural but functional design
  • Private bathroom
  • Breakfast buffet
